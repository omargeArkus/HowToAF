Beta Onboarding Guide

Get started with
ActiveFit+ Beta

Follow the steps below to install TestFlight, set up ActiveFit+, and activate your beta access.

Phase 1 — Installing TestFlight
01
1
Open the App Store on your iOS device.
2
Tap the Search tab at the bottom right and type "TestFlight".
3
Tap the download icon (or "Get") to install the official Apple TestFlight app.
4
Once installation is complete, tap Open.
5
When prompted, choose Allow or Don't Allow notifications — allowing is recommended to get alerts about new beta builds.
6
Read the welcome screen and tap Accept on the Terms and Conditions.
1 of 5
Phase 2 — Installing ActiveFit+
02
⚡ You can skip this phase if ActiveFit+ is already installed on your device.
1
Open the App Store on your iOS device.
2
Tap the Search tab and type "Active Fit +".
3
Tap the download icon (or "Get") to install the app.
4
Once installation is complete, tap Open.
5
Access the app with valid credentials and complete the onboarding process.
2 of 5
Phase 3 — Activating the Beta Request
03
1
Launch your current version of ActiveFit+ and open the main menu.
2
Go to Settings → Advanced Settings.
3
Scroll down to the Send Logs section at the bottom.
4
Tap Join Beta Program — you'll be automatically redirected to TestFlight.
3 of 5
Phase 4 — Installing the Beta Build
04
1
In TestFlight, tap Continue on the "From the Developer" and "Share Feedback" screens.
2
Tap the Install button next to the app icon.
3
If prompted about replacing the standard version, tap Install again.
⚠ Note: You may lose current app data.
4
Wait for the download to finish, then tap Open to start testing the new version.
4 of 5

💡 Helpful Tips

Learn how to take screenshots and record your screen on iPhone — useful for sharing feedback with the team.

How to Take a Screenshot
1
Open the app or screen you want to capture.
2
Press the Side Button + Volume Up at the same time.
3
Release quickly — the screen flashes and a thumbnail appears bottom-left.
4
Tap the thumbnail to edit or swipe it away to save to Photos.
📱 Note: On older iPhones with a Home button (iPhone SE, iPhone 8 and earlier), press the Side Button and the Home button simultaneously instead.
How to Record your Screen
1
Go to Settings → Control Center and add Screen Recording.
2
Swipe down from the top-right corner to open Control Center.
3
Tap the Screen Recording button — a 3-second countdown starts.
4
To stop, tap the red bar at the top and tap Stop. Video saves to Photos.
🎙 Long-press the button and enable Microphone to record with audio.
