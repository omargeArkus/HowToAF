iOS Beta Onboarding Guide

Get started with
ActiveFit+ on iPhone

Follow the steps below to install the beta version via Firebase App Distribution on your iPhone.

Phase 1 — Opening the Invitation Email
01
1
On your iPhone, open Safari and tap the button below to access the beta download page. Download ActiveFit+ Beta
⚠ You must use Safari — other browsers do not support the profile installation process on iOS.
2
Enter your Google account email address in the email field and submit.
⚠ You must use the Google account that received the beta invitation — other accounts will be denied access.
3
Open your email app and look for the invitation email from Firebase. Open it directly on your iPhone.
💡 You may receive two invitation emails — one for iOS and one for Android. Make sure to open the iOS invitation to proceed correctly.
4
Locate the button labeled "Get Started" and tap it. Safari will open the Firebase App Distribution download page.
Main menu
Phase 2 — Installing Firebase App Distribution
02
1
Safari opens the Firebase App Distribution page showing the app name and version. Keep this page open.
💡 If the page does not load correctly, make sure you are using Safari — other browsers may not support profile installation on iOS.
2
Sign in with your Google account to verify your access to the build.
3
Read and accept the Terms and Conditions when prompted.
4
Tap "Register Device" or "Download Profile" to continue.
5
A system popup will appear: "This website is trying to download a configuration profile." Tap Allow.
6
Once the profile is downloaded, close the "Profile Downloaded" notification and proceed to the next phase.
Main menu
Phase 3 — Trust the Profile
03
1
Open your iPhone Settings app.
2
Tap "Profile Downloaded" at the top of Settings — or navigate to General > VPN & Device Management.
3
Select the Firebase Software Distribution profile from the list.
4
Tap "Install" in the top right corner.
5
Enter your passcode when prompted, then tap Install again to confirm.
Main menu
Phase 4 — Download the App
04
1
Return to Safari and go back to the Firebase App Distribution page — or tap the "App Tester" icon if it was added to your Home Screen.
2
Find the app version you were invited to test and tap "Download" or "Install".
3
✓ All done! Wait for the app icon to appear on your Home Screen. Once it finishes loading, the beta app is ready to use.
